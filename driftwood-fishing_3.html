HOW TO FISH

it's the easy part of life
1. Cast — hold SPACE / mouse to charge, release to cast. Distance matters: some fish hug the shallows, others sit way out.
2. Wait — small taps are just nibbles. Don't strike! Wait for the ! and the big tug, then press to hook.
3. Reel — hold to raise the catch bar, release to drop it. Keep the fish inside the bar to fill the line. Bigger rods, bigger bar.
4. Live by the water — seasons turn every few days and the weather has moods. Rain wakes the fish, fog stirs strange ones. Check the Request Board for daily jobs, and mount your best catches in the Cabin (each trophy boosts your sale prices).
5. Read the water — dark shapes cruising under the surface are real fish: cast near one to target it. Bubbling patches mean fast bites. Nail the gold sliver on the cast meter for a perfect cast, and keep landing fish without a loss to build a 🔥 streak bonus.
Tips — fish care about time of day, season, weather, distance and lures. The Fishopedia tracks what you've learned. Coins buy rods, lures, gear and travel passes. Progress saves automatically.
